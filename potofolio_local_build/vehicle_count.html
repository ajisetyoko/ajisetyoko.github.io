Vehicle Detection and Counting Systems using YOLO Deep Learning Framework

數位影像處理 Digital Image Processing Final Task | 2018 | Course ID EE5412701
Aji Setyoko,  Hendrik Tampubolon 
National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, Taiwan

Abstract


The real time vehicle count is necessary to know the real-time traffic monitoring as the additional information of Global Positioning System data. A simple tracking method is combine with Vehicle detection based on real-time object detection to produce very robust, very light, and high accuration real-time vehicle counter. Region of Interest and High-Pass Filter method is also employed to give a high reliability of the model. By tested the model on real traffic monitoring video shown the model get 99% accuracy.
